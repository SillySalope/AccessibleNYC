Accessible NYC

A look into how NYC Transit challenges seniors and disabled persons.

Find out more

NYC Public Transit is essential, but if you use a wheelchair, it may be out of reach!

On 2016, 57% of New Yorkers used Public Transit to commute to work1
and there was a daily average of +5 million trips on the subway, and +2 million on buses. 2
.1
However, 10% of NYC's population has some disability, and 13% of population is above the age of 65. How bumpy is the commute for millions of disabled and senior New Yorkers?
